Causes for your dog to be Friendly.


In looking at overly friendly dogs, researchers found that they carry two variants of genes called GTF2I and GTF2IRD1. Those are the same genes that, when absent in human beings, cause Williams syndrome—a condition in which there’s a surplus of oxytocin, a.k.a. the love hormone.

How to tell that your dog is Friendly?
  1. Ears erect and pointed forward
  2. Smiling and looking a bit sheepish
  3. Whips its body side to side
  4. Wagging its tail
  5. Cuddles against your legs
  6. Face looks relaxed
  7. It may make little happy barks or yips
